TRADING CHART

A trading chart, also known as a price chart or stock chart, is a graphical representation of the price movement of a financial instrument over a specific period of time. It is commonly used by traders and investors to analyze the historical price data of stocks, commodities, currencies, and other assets in order to make informed trading decisions.

Trading charts typically display the price on the vertical axis (y-axis) and the time on the horizontal axis (x-axis). The price can be represented in various formats, such as line charts, bar charts, or candlestick charts. Trading charts often include additional technical indicators and overlays, such as moving averages, volume bars, and trendlines, to provide further insights into the price action and potential trading opportunities.
